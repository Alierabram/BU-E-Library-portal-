Help Center & FAQs

Quick answers to commonly asked questions.

How do I renew my borrowed books online? +

Log in to your account management section below, view your active loans, and click the 'Renew' button next to the eligible items.

What is the maximum number of books I can borrow? +

Undergraduate students can borrow up to 5 books for 14 days. Graduate students and Faculty have extended limits of 10 and 15 books respectively.

How can I access the library databases from home? +

Use the 'Remote Access' link under our Services page. You will need to log in with your university credentials to establish a secure connection.
